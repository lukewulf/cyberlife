About

At CyberLife, we love plants. And, you should not have to worry about taking care of your plants on a daily basis. Consistently, we found that people forget to water their plants, accidentally killing them in the process. We believe that all people should be able to enjoy Nature's beauty without the hassle and burden of a fixed watering schedule. Plants should no longer shrivel and die on your windowsill, and instead thrive in the most remote of places.

CyberLife is a wonderful addition to any green-thumb hobbyist's home. With our unique web application and portable design, you should no longer worry about keeping your hard-to-reach plants alive. With a simple click of a button and a WiFi connection, you will be able to remotely water your plants from the comfort of your phone, completely reducing the need to fill up a watering can and making the rounds around your garden. Also, with our integrated water sensor, we will notify you when you need to refill the water supply to your CyberLife pot. With CyberLife, the homeplant will enter the 21st century.

Our Origin

CyberLife was founded by four UC San Diego college students. As the final project for ECE196: Hands On Project Building, we have worked to create an IoT solution to what we believe is a common problem using our knowledge of hardware, software and mechanical design. The initial prototype of the web application and CyberLife pot were created in 5 weeks, and we are constantly finding ways to improve our software and hardware designs. Through this project, we have learned the important skills of full-stack web development, python programming and database management through Google Firebase.
